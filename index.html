South Central Kentucky Orthopedics

South Central Kentucky Orthopedics has been committed to providing state-of-the-art musculoskeletal care for over 30 years. Our primary goals are patient satisfaction and safety. We strive to achieve the best outcome for every patient and to restore quality of life without pain. Our board-certified orthopedic surgeons and staff are committed to giving compassionate and comprehensive care to patients in the communities we serve.

Southern Kentucky Orthopedic Rehab Team
Southern Kentucky Orthopedic Rehab (our motto is "Patient First, Team Always." Our focus is Patient Centered Rehabilitation, customizing your therapy plan and treatment to meet your specific diagnosis and goals. We care about you, the individual, and it will show. Quality of therapy received at SKOR is exceptional, and should be expected by you the patient. We accomplish this high level of care with experienced therapists, well rooted in the practice of orthopedic based therapy. SKOR offers Physical Therapy services. About Your Visit

Insurance Information

We accept most major insurance plans.

Appointments

Please call the office to make an appointment in advance. If you are unable to keep your appointment, please call us as far in advance as possible so we may use that time to see another patient in need of care. We make a sincere effort to adhere to our appointment schedule and appreciate your patience if we are late due to emergencies or hospital surgery.

Fees & Payments

We make every effort to decrease the cost of your medical care. Therefore, we request payment arrangements for all office services at the time they are rendered unless prior arrangements have been made. We accept cash, checks, MasterCard, Visa, Discover and American Express for your convenience. If we are a participating provider of your insurance company, we will bill them. However, payment is the patient's responsibility. We will help in any way we can to assist you in handling claims.
